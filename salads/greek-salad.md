# Greek salad recipe


## Ingredients

**Recipe for 4 people**

- 2 big tomatos (or 4 smaller tomatos)
- 1 big cucumber (or 2 to 3 smaller cucumbers)
- 1/2 big red onion (or 1 small red onion)
- 1/2 green bell pepper
- 200g feta cheese
- 1 table spoon of red grape vinegar (or balsamic vinegar)
- 2 table spoons of olive oil
- 1 tea spoon of salt
- 1 tea spoon of oregano


## Instructions

1. **Prepare the Vegetables:**
   - Thoroughly wash the vegrtables
   - Chop the vegetables in small pieces (tomatos and cucumber) and stripes (bell pepper and onion)

2. **Assemble the Salad:**
   - In a large bowl combine the vegetables
   - Add the salt, the vinegar, the olive oil and the oregano
   - Mix well
   - Add the salad in a big plate
   - Place the feta cheese on top (whole)
   - Add a drizzel of olive oil and a pinch of oregano on top (optional)

3. **Serve:**
   - Serve the salad immediately, ensuring it's fresh and crisp.
