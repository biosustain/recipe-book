Brownie with Chocolate Cream#

Ingredients#

  • 5 eggs

  • 500 g sugar

  • 125 g all-purpose flour

  • 100 g cocoa powder

  • 250 g melted butter

  • 30 g roughly chopped dark chocolate

Cream:#

  • 200 g sifted powdered sugar

  • 75 g room-temperature butter

  • 30 g sifted cocoa powder

  • 150 g cold cream cheese

Instructions#

  1. Preheat the oven to 170¬∞C (not fan-assisted).

  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the flour and cocoa powder, and whisk until everything is well combined.

  4. Pour in the melted butter and stir to combine.

  5. Manually mix in the dark chocolate.

  6. Spread the batter into a baking pan (about 30 √ó 25 cm) lined with baking paper.

  7. Bake for 30‚Äì35 minutes, or until the top is set but the brownie is still soft in the middle.

  8. Let the brownie cool completely.

For the cream:#

  1. Whisk together the powdered sugar, butter, and cocoa at medium speed until well combined and smooth.

  2. Add the cream cheese all at once and mix until fully incorporated.

  3. Increase to medium-high speed and beat until the frosting is light and fluffy, at least 5 minutes ‚Äî but be careful not to overmix.

  4. Spread the frosting over the brownie once it‚Äôs completely cool.
