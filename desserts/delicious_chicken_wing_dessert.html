  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1 tsp smoked paprika

  • 1 tsp garlic powder

  2. Cook Wings:

    • Stir constantly for 1-2 minutes until the glaze thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon. Remove from heat.
