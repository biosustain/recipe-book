# Marry me Pasta 

## Ingredients 
- penne (500g)
- sundried tomatos (you decide) 
- onion (1pcs)
- heavy cream (200ml)
- spinach (you decide)
- garlic (2 cloves)
- grana padano
- chili flakes, salt, pepper, oregano (pinch)


## Instructions
 
### PASTA
1. Boil pasta for 1 minute less than it says on the package 
2. When done, strain pasta but save a cup of pasta water.

### SAUCE
1. Chop onion
2. Fry onion on the sundreid tomato oil 
3. Chop sundried tomatos and add them 
4. Add pressed garlic 
5. Add chili flakes and oregano 
6. Add cream 
7. Simmer until cream turns red 
8. Add spinach (fresh or frozen)
9. Add shredded grana padano to the sauce
10. Salt and pepper to taste 
11. Add your pasta and cook in the sauce until pasta is al dente 
12. Add pasta water (couple of teaspoons)
13. EAT LIKE A KING
